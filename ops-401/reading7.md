# Questions?

**1.From your day to day computer use, provide examples of when data is at rest and when it is in transit.** 
Data in my hardrive are data at rest. When I move them over the internet, it is consider data in transit. Likely I will be uploading photos or papers to the cloud. 

**2. Explain the role data encryption with regards to the CIA triad.** 
Encryption is a great way to protect data at rest and in transit. There are secure connections to moving data like HTTPS, SSL, TLS, and FTPS. Hardrives can also be encrypted for data at rest.
